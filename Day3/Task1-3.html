Semantic Elements

Header

Header represents the top section of a webpage or a content block, usually containing a title, logo, or introductory information.

Nav

Nav is used to group navigation links together in a meaningful way. It tells search engines and screen readers that this part of the page controls navigation.

Main

Main holds the primary and most important content of the webpage. It helps assistive technologies skip repetitive parts like headers and nav bars.

Article

Article represents a self-contained piece of content that can stand alone, such as a blog post, news item, or review. Even if taken out of the page, it still makes sense by itself.
